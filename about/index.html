About Us

We are JamCris Trading & Machine Surplus

Founded by three siblings named Jimmy, Alex, and Mercy Crisostomo in 2003, the company formerly known as "Almers" has been providing quality products and services for 20 years now. The name "JAMCRIS" was a clever combination of the siblings' names and it has become synonymous with excellence and reliability in their industry.

Today, our shop is located at Anilao, Malolos, Bulacan and it has become a go-to destination for customers looking for top-notch products and services.

Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality car auto parts and trading machine surplus products to our customers, while delivering exceptional customer service and building lasting relationships with our clients.

Vision

We aim to be a one-stop-shop for all our customer's needs and continuously expand our product offerings to meet their evolving requirements.
