About Us

We are JamCris Trading & Machine Surplus

Founded by three siblings named Jimmy, Alex, and Mercy Crisostomo in 2003, the company formerly known as "Almers" has been providing quality products and services for 20 years now. The name "JAMCRIS" was a clever combination of the siblings' names and it has become synonymous with excellence and reliability in their industry. Today, our shop is located at Anilao, Malolos, Bulacan and it has become a go-to destination for customers looking for top-notch products and services.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to provide high-quality machines and equipment to our clients while maintaining a commitment to customer satisfaction and service.

Our vision is to become the leading provider of industrial machines and equipment in the region, delivering cutting-edge technology and innovative solutions to our clients.
